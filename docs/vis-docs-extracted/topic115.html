Volley Live Challenge Type

Type of a challenge for the live management of a volleyball match.

 Values

The following table lists the values for the outcomes.

Value

 Name Description

1

 AntennaTouch Antenna touch: contact with the antenna by the player or by the ball.

2

 AttackLineFault Faulty contact of a player with the 3-meter attack line.

3

 BallInOut Ball in/out; for side and end lines.

4

 BlockTouch Block touch: contact with the ball by the player (i.e. the blocker).

5

 CenterLineFault The complete crossing of the centre line by the player's foot or feet.

6

 NetTouch Net fault: the contact with the net between the antennae by the player in the action of playing the ball.

7

 ServiceLineFault Contact of the server with the playing court (end line included) or the lateral free zone outside the service zone.

8

 FloorTouch Floor touch.
