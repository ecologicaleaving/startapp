Volleyball Match Format

Format of a volleyball match.

Indicates the number of sets of the match, the number of points to win a set and the points different to win a set.

The following table lists the values for the formats for a volleyball match.

Value

 Name Description

0

 Unknown Unknown.

1

 Set5_Points25_Rally
  • 5 sets: a team must win 3 sets to win the match.
  • Sets 1 to 4: at least 25 points, with at least 2 points of difference to win.
  • Set 5: at least 15 points, with at least 2 points of difference to win.

2

 Set7_Points15
  • 7 sets: a team must win 4 sets to win the match.
  • At least 15 points for each set.
  • At least 2 points of difference to win a set.
