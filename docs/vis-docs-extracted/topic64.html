Beach Match Format

Format for a beach volleyball match.

 Values

The following table lists the values for the formats for a beach volleyball match.

Value

 Name Description

1

 Set3-Points12
  • 3 sets: a team must win 2 sets to win the match.
  • Sets 1 and 2: 12 points.
  • Set 3: at least 12 points, with at least 2 points of difference to win.

2

 Set3-Points21
  • 3 sets: a team must win 2 sets to win the match.
  • At least 21 points for each set.
  • At least 2 points of difference to win a set.

3

 Set3-Points25-15
  • 3 sets: a team must win 2 sets to win the match.
  • Sets 1 and 2: at least 25 points.
  • Set 3: at least 15 points.
  • At least 2 points of difference to win a set.

4

 Set3-Points21-15
  • 3 sets: a team must win 2 sets to win the match.
  • Sets 1 and 2: at least 21 points.
  • Set 3: at least 15 points.
  • At least 2 points of difference to win a set.

5

 Set1-Points15
  • 1 set.
  • 15 points.
  • At least 2 points of difference to win a set.

6

 Set5-Points11
  • 5 sets: a team must win 3 sets to win the match.
  • 11 points.
  • At least 2 points of difference to win a set.

14

 Set5-Points15-7
  • 5 sets: a team must win 3 sets to win the match.
  • Sets 1 to 4: at least 15 points.
  • Sets 5: at least 7 points.
  • At least 2 points of difference to win a set.

72

 Set3-Points15
  • 3 sets: a team must win 2 sets to win the match.
  • At least 15 points for each set.
  • At least 2 points of difference to win a set.

75

 Set3-Points11
  • 3 sets: a team must win 2 sets to win the match.
  • At least 11 points for each set.
  • At least 2 points of difference to win a set.
